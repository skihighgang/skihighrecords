SkiHigh Records: A record label that takes your music to the next level

We are SkiHigh Records, a record label founded by SkiHighMarco, a talented rapper and producer

Get Started

Get started with SkiHigh Records today: It’s easy and fun

SkiHigh Records is a record label that offers you the opportunity to work with talented and experienced professionals, access to high-quality recording and distribution services, and a supportive and creative community of artists and fans.

Sign a fair and flexible contract with SkiHigh Records that respects your rights and interests as an artist.

You will receive a competitive royalty rate, a reasonable advance, and a non-exclusive deal that allows you to pursue other opportunities.

Access a range of services and tools that will help you create, distribute, and promote your music.

You will get professional recording and mixing, digital distribution to all major platforms, marketing and publicity support, and more.

Services

Check out the great services we offer

Talent Scouting.

We are always on the lookout for the next rap star. We scout various venues, platforms, genres, and scenes to find and sign the most promising and talented artists in the game.

Contract Negotiation.

We offer our artists fair and flexible contracts that respect their rights and interests. We negotiate the best terms for them, such as royalty rates, advances, recoupment, distribution, promotion, ownership, duration, and termination.

Artist Development.

We help our artists grow their skills, creativity, and fanbase. We provide them with feedback, support, guidance, resources, and opportunities. We also help them set and achieve their musical goals.

Global Exposure.

We distribute and promote our artists’ music to various platforms and media around the world. We use a distribution service such as LANDR or DistroKid to simplify this process. We also create press kits, send out press releases, pitch to media outlets, run ads, create merchandising, organize events, and engage with fans.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What kind of rap music do you release?

    Mostly rap.

  • How do you find and sign new artists?

    We find and sign new artists by scouting various music venues, platforms, genres, and scenes. We also accept submissions and demos from artists who want to join our label. We look for artists who have talent, potential, and passion for rap music.

  • What are the benefits of joining SkiHigh Records?

    Joining SkiHigh Records means becoming part of a family of music lovers and makers who share a passion for innovation and excellence. You will enjoy the freedom and flexibility to create your own music, while benefiting from the guidance and resources of our expert team. You will also get access to high-quality recording and distribution services, marketing and publicity support, and a supportive and creative community of artists and fans.

  • How do you distribute and promote your artists’ music?

    We distribute and promote our artists’ music to various platforms and media around the world. We use a distribution service such as LANDR or DistroKid to simplify this process. We also create press kits, send out press releases, pitch to media outlets, run ads, create merchandising, organize events, and engage with fans.

  • How do you calculate and pay royalties to your artists?

    We calculate and pay royalties to our artists based on the terms of their contracts. We offer our artists competitive royalty rates, reasonable advances, and transparent accounting. We pay our artists on a quarterly basis through direct deposit or BTC.

  • How can I contact SkiHigh Records?

    You can contact SkiHigh Records by email at skihighrecs@gmail.com
